## Challenge

A heritage luxury fashion house needed to modernize their digital presence while maintaining brand prestige. Their existing platform suffered from poor performance, outdated UX, and low conversion rates.

## Solution

We architected a headless commerce solution using Next.js 15, integrating with their existing inventory systems while creating a premium, performant shopping experience.

### Technical Approach

- **Frontend**: Next.js 15 with App Router, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS
- **Backend**: Headless CMS with real-time inventory sync
- **Performance**: Edge caching, image optimization, 98 Lighthouse score
- **Personalization**: AI-driven product recommendations

### Design Philosophy

Every interaction was crafted to reflect the brand's luxury positioning. We implemented:

- Micro-interactions with Framer Motion
- Custom cursor effects
- Smooth scroll with Lenis
- Premium typography and spacing

## Results

Within 6 months of launch:

- **340% increase** in online revenue
- **156% higher** conversion rate
- **98/100** Lighthouse performance score
- **2.3s** average page load time

## Impact

The platform now serves as the brand's primary revenue channel, with mobile commerce accounting for 67% of transactions. Customer satisfaction scores increased by 89%.
